Entry 01 — About Us

Built on integrity. Operated with discipline.

Phanica Security Services Ltd is a private limited company registered in Kenya in 2025, with its operations focused on the manned guarding and protection segment. We are dedicated to offering solutions that add genuine value to our clients at competitive rates.

As private security providers, we do far more than detect and report crime. We work closely with every client to understand their risks, then build a response that keeps people, property and operations safe — while helping control costs and protect profitability.

With operations established across Kisii, Nyamira, Migori & Homabay Counties and expanding, we're building long-term partnerships rather than transactional contracts. Our clients' priorities become our priorities.

Entry 02 — Services

Deployment capabilities

From standing guard posts to specialist patrols, every deployment is backed by tailored personnel selection, training and a written standard operating procedure (SOP) specific to your site.

01

Manned Guarding

Unarmed day and night guards stationed at gates, receptions and grounds — trained on site-specific SOPs, occurrence books and incident reporting.

02

Canine Security

Outsourced canine patrol teams pairing a licensed, certified handler with a trained, socialised working dog for high-profile venues.

03

Fire Watch Patrol

Trained guards monitor for fire and life-safety hazards when alarm or suppression systems are impaired, logging continuous patrols of high-risk areas.

04

Temporary & Event Security

Short-term and event-based deployments scaled to your timeline, with the same screening and training standards as our permanent posts.

05

Security Surveys & Audits

On-site risk assessments with practical, ongoing recommendations — the starting point for any new guarding contract.

06

Patrol Vehicles & Motorcycles

Mobile response units supporting static guard posts, enabling our teams to respond within the shortest time possible.

How an engagement runs

SOP & CLIENT REVIEW CYCLE
STEP 01

Survey & SOP draft

Site survey, tailored manpower selection, and a draft standard operating procedure covering emergency and site-specific instructions.

STEP 02

Deployment & handover

Approved SOPs, uniforms and equipment go live. Shift handovers and instructions are logged in the occurrence book (OB).

STEP 03

Reporting

Incident reports are issued to the client with a copy retained on site, including improvement recommendations where relevant.

STEP 04

Monthly review

Scheduled client meetings cover risk assessments, site audits, KPIs, refresher training and a monthly scorecard / SLA review.
